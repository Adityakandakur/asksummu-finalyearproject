Where Engineering Meets Real Impact Three Proposals. One Team. One Vision.

We are a team of final-year engineering students from Padre Conceição College of Engineering, Goa University. This website presents our final-year project proposals for the academic year 2025–2026. Our goal is to design innovative and practical technology solutions that solve real-world problems and create a positive impact on society. Each project is developed through research, teamwork, and engineering knowledge. These projects reflect our effort to build useful, affordable, and smart systems that can help people and improve everyday life.

SparshaX
A refreshable Braille display built from first principles — replacing a $100 piezoelectric actuator with a cam, a micro-magnet, and a hand-wound coil. Under ₹80 per cell. AI camera, Bluetooth, and a built-in learning mode for first-time Braille users.
