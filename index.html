About
about asksummu

asksummu

asksummu is a team of final-year engineers working at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Smart Automation. We believe transformative technology should not be a privilege — it must be accessible to every student, every farmer, and every person who stands to benefit from it.

Our approach is grounded in first principles: identify problems that matter, engineer solutions that are cost-effective and reproducible, and build systems that operate reliably in the real world — not only in controlled laboratory conditions.

Our Work
P · 01  ·  Accessibility Technology
⠿
SparshaX

A refreshable Braille display rebuilt from the ground up. The ₹8,000+ piezoelectric actuator is replaced by a cam, a micro-magnet, and a self-wound coil — producing a full Braille cell for under ₹80, manufacturable in any college fabrication lab. Equipped with Bluetooth text input, AI-powered OCR for reading printed material, and a progressive learning mode.

HardwareAccessibilityAI · OCRBluetooth
P · 02  ·  Precision Agriculture
KhetRakshak

A solar-powered autonomous drone stationed permanently in the field. It eliminates weeds via a focused laser — no GPS, no chemicals — monitors crop health through computer vision, and delivers farm intelligence to any Indian farmer via SMS on a basic 2G handset. No internet connection, no smartphone, no external dependency required.
