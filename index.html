About
about asksummu

asksummu

asksummu is a team of final-year engineers operating at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Smart Automation. We believe that transformative technology should not be a privilege — it should be within reach of the student, the farmer, and the everyday person.

Our mission is straightforward: identify real problems, engineer first-principles solutions, and deliver outcomes that are affordable, accessible, and built to last. Every proposal we undertake is grounded in that commitment.

⠿ SparshaX

A refreshable Braille display rebuilt from the ground up — replacing the $100 piezoelectric actuator with a cam, a micro-magnet, and a self-wound coil. The result is a full Braille cell manufacturable for under ₹80 in any college lab, complete with Bluetooth connectivity, AI-powered OCR, and a built-in learning mode for first-time Braille users.

KhetRakshak

A solar-powered autonomous farm drone designed to live permanently in the field. It eliminates weeds using a focused laser — no GPS, no chemicals — monitors crop health via computer vision, and delivers actionable farm intelligence to any Indian farmer via SMS on a basic 2G handset. No internet, no smartphone, no external dependency.
