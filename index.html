SparshaX
A refreshable Braille display built from first principles — replacing a $100 piezoelectric actuator with a cam, a micro-magnet, and a hand-wound coil. Under ₹80 per cell. AI camera, Bluetooth, and a built-in learning mode for first-time Braille users.
